Meet Surveyor AZEEZ SALEEM ADEWALE

Meet Surveyor AZEEZ SALEEM ADEWALE,

Principal Partner

Mnis Azeez Saleem Adewale (ASA), Mnis was born into the family of Late Paa Azeez Ajagbe and Alhaja Bintu Azeez at Agbegi compound, Oke Bola, Ikire Oyo State (now Osun State). ASA’s early education was at Nawair-Ud-Deen Primary School where he obtained his First School Leaving Certificate as one of the best pupilsin his class which earned him admission into Baptist Grammar School where he obtained his Junior and Senior Secondary SchoolCertifications. During his senior school education, he was admitted into the Arts class as he intended to study Law when completed his secondary school. Although he earned a very good grade but due to lack of funds he couldn’t proceed to the university at that time, so he was compelled to spend some time with his mummy (Alhaja) at ikire before he travelled to Lagos state so as to chart a course for his life .This amiable and focused man, started his working career with Empee textile company at Oshodi as a casual worker for two years before he noticed that he could not achieve his dream as a lawyer if he continued with the casual work. Meanwhile, then in his dreams most of the time he found himself with people measuring land at various places which became very worrisome to him as he could not understand the meaning until he got a place around Muritala Airport, Ikeja where he saw a team of surveyors working on the road then he recalled his dream and started working on it .ASA then had a friend whose brother was a survey Assistant, so, he decided to quit the factory job and started learning survey which he he learned for about four years before he graduated and started his new career of working as a survey assistant. Being that Azeez Saleem Adewale is an Education loving person and has a passion for learning, after working as a survey assistant he decided to enroll himself for a Remedial Programme because he had fallen in love with the survey profession for which he did not have the O'level requirements to study as earlier, he completed his secondary school education as an Arts student. In 2009,Azeez Saleem Adewale sat for another WAEC as a science student and once again cleared all his papers which qualified him to sit for JAMB in 2011 and scored 201 marks to secure his admission into Federal Polytechnic, llaro in 2012 where he bagged an Ordinary National Diploma is Surveying and Geo-Informatics with Upper credit. ASA performed his one year industrial attachment (IT) at Geo-Plus consult, Lagos in 2014-2015 In 2015, he proceeded for his Higher National Diploma in the same Surveying and Geo-informatics and graduated in 2017 with Distinction and also as the Best student at the Faculty of Environmental Science, Federal Polytechnic Ilaro, Ogun state, Nigeria with awards and gifts. This focused man still believed that he had not achieved his dream, so, in 2018, he sat for Technologists Examination under SURVEYORS COUNCIL OF NIGERIA and he passed his all papers which elevated him to PUPIL SURVEYOR OF THE FEDERAL REPUBLIC OF NIGERIA in 2019. He observed his pupilage under DATAVIEW CONSULT LIMITED from 2019 – 2021 In 2018, he gained a direct entry admission at BELL UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY, OTA to study Surveying and Geo-informatics thereby obtaining his First Degree in 2020 In 2021, Azeez Saleem Adewale proceeded to write his final professional examination in Abuja also under SURVERORS COUNCIL OF NIGERIA. All the papers were passed and he became a REGISTERED SURVEYOR OF THE FEDERAL REPUBLIC OF NIGERIA and was inducted in March 2022. Earlier in 2021, he was again admitted into BELL UNIVERSITY OF TECHNOLOGY, OTA for Masters Degree to study M.Sc in PROJECT MANAGEMENT which he also competed successfully in 2022. He owns a private practice organization called Geo-Mapping Consults LTD and is a reputable corporate member of NIGERIAN INSTITUTION OF SURVEYORS as well as PRIVATE PRACTISING SURVEROYS OF NIGERIA.
